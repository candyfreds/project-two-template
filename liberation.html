November 7th - 10th 2019

Liberation Rock Camp (LRC) is a weekend retreat in Portland, OR that provides an opportunity for women, transgender and gender-expansive folk 21 and over to let loose for the weekend and create original music and community together.

How it Works

You’ll learn an instrument (guitar, bass, drums, vocals), form a band, write an original song, and perform live for all your screaming fans, in just 2 1/2 days. NO MUSICAL EXPERIENCE NECESSARY! We provide all the instruments and amazing instructors, along with skill-based and empowerment workshops. Plus meals and an outrageous KARAOKE PARTY!!

Itinerary

Thursday, Nov 7th – 6pm – 9pm: Meet & Greet, Dinner & Welcome, Song Writing Workshop, Band Formation Friday, Nov 8th – 6pm – 9pm: Potluck dinner with performance by Piefight!, Instrument Instruction Saturday, Nov 9th – 9am – 11pm: Breakfast, Instrument Instruction, Lunch & Pedal Demo, Band Practice, Dinner & performance TBA – KARAOKE PARTY! Sunday, Nov 10th – 10:30am – 8:30 pm: Band Practice, Lunch & performance TBA, more Band Practice, Showcase Prep, dinner on your own, Showcase!
