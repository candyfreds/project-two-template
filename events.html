Liberation Rock Camp

November 7 - November 10 Liberation Rock Camp (LRC) is a weekend retreat in Portland from Nov 7-10 that provides an opportunity for women and transgender and gender-expansive folk 21 and over to create original music and community together. Visit the program page for more information!
