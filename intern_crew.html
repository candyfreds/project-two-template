Become an Intern!

If you’ve been a camper at least 2 times and are 15 years old or older you are eligible to apply to our Intern Crew. During summer camp our Interns work closely with our campers as peer mentors, teaching them all the things you learned while being a camper. Instrument Instruction, Band Manager, Roadie Gear, Manager Camper Chaos, Teach Workshops, Perform Skits! You’ll learn leadership skills, how to work with youth,and make amazing friends. You’ll even have a chance to form an Intern Band and perform at Showcase!
